# Notes from the Blurgatory

Somewhere between the fizzlecrag and the hollow nubbin, a
committee of squonks convened to discuss the missing spindle.
No conclusions were reached. None were expected.

1. Locate the spindle, or a suitable facsimile
2. Reconvene under the drooping yarnwood
3. Blame the nearest gronch

```
while (spindle == null) {
  squabble();
  postpone();
}
```

The committee adjourns until further nonsense.
