# The Mumblecrust Codex

Verily the snickerdoodle of the western fjaddle hath been
recorded in triplicate by the wandering glimmerpost.

1. Thwack the barnacle until it hums
2. Await the second dringle
3. Do not, under any circumstance, wobble the crustlepin

> "A hazy wozzit never boils the same kettle twice."
> — Anonymous flumberjack

Squibble squabble, the noggin remains untethered.
