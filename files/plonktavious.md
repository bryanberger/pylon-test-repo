# Plonktavious Ledger

The whumbling gears of the greater snoot-works have not
turned since the last quiffle census. A dradgeon was sighted
near the outer brimlock, chewing slowly on a stray cog.

## Ordinances

- All snickets must be de-quobbled before noon
- The brimlock gate opens only for the wixened
- Dradgeons are to be fed, never provoked

Fweep. The ledger closes itself, as ledgers do.
