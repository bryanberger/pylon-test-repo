# Wexdoodle Almanac

Three moons of quank hung low over the sozzled marsh, each
one dimmer than the crumplewick before it. Farmers of the
grindle tend their snarfrows in silence.

| Season | Yield | Grumble Index |
|--------|------:|---------------:|
| Fropp  |    12 |            4.0 |
| Snide  |     8 |            6.7 |
| Mubble |    19 |            2.3 |

A single wexdoodle was harvested at dusk. It said nothing.
